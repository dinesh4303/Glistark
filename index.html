Why Choose A 1:1 Online Architect Consultation?

  • You aren’t sure whether your ideas and vision will work
  • You need drawings of your design in order for it to get costed up by a builder
  • You aren’t sure which finishes to choose for your room
  • You need your design on paper so that you can communicate your ideas effectively to a tradie

During your one-on-one video call, you will:

  • Speak with an architect that has over a decade of experience
  • Get personalised advice about your ideas and see them translated and implemented in your design
  • Obtain a layout that makes use of the space efficiently
  • Gain advice about finishes and fixtures
  • Get access to trusted suppliers
